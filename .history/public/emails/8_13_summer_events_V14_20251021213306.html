Inclusion For All!
August 23rd, 8:30 am - 10:00 am

Low Sensory Mornings are a special time for individuals with sensory sensitivities and other needs, to visit and explore the park before the hustle and bustle of our crowds. Though open early, the event will still provide access to animal feedings.

The park will open at 8:30 am to our special guests, and then open to the public at 10:00 am; stay as long as you wish!

No preregistration required; group rates not applicable. Regular rates apply, Free with Park Membership.
